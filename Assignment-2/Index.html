  3. (Nested Control Statement) A college offers a course that prepares students for the state licensing exam for real estate brokers. Next to each name is written a 1 if the student passed the exam and a 2 if the student failed. Your program should analyze the results of the exam as follows:

    • Count the number of test results of each type.
    • Display a summary of the test results indicating the number of students who passed and the number of students who failed.
    • If more than eight students passed the exam, print the message “Raise tuition.”

  10. The process of finding the largest value (i.e. the maximum of a group of values) is used frequently in computer applications.   12. A company wants to transmit data over the telephone, but it is concerned that its phones may be tapped. All of its data is transmitted as four-digit integers. It has asked you to write a program that will encrypt its data so that the data may be transmitted more securely. Then swap the first digit with the third, and swap the second digit with the fourth. Then output text that displays the encrypted integer.
